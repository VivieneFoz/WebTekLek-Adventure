INDIGENTS

The usual people which they considered “Indigents” are those who are called “Four Piece”. The Meaning of Four Piece is the scholars and those people who are included in the “Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program” which was introduced by Late President Pinoy Aquino. The activities that are involved here are usually under the part of livelihood that includes actual cooking demonstration. The wife of the head captain of barangay is also a part of the livelihood sector which teaches the art craft.
